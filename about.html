About Me

My name is Salem Saberhagen and I am a 500-year-old witch who has been sentenced to spend 100 years as a cat by the Witches Council in an outrageous miscarriage of justice. They call this my 'punishment' for my completely innocent attempt to take over the world.

They have sent me to live with Hilda Spellman because she supported my regime as a part of the refreshments committee, and so they feel (and I agree) that she must continue her act of catering to my every whim.

Don't let the negative press fool you. I would have made a kind, fair, wise world leader, and the world suffers my loss to this day.
